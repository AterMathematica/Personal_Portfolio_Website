Special Interests

Amidst all the academics I pursue, I also make time to enjoy my passions, which include theatre, building, caring for my reptiles, fiber arts, and a variety of other interests.

Theatre

I have been an active part of Moscow Community Theatre since I was nine when I started as an actor. I have since delved into many different areas of the theatre, including the technical side, house management, stage management, and poster creation.


Posters

Each of these posters was created by myself and the show's directory in a collaborative design process. My goal when creating each poster was to effectively capture the director's vision. I did this by soliciting feedback and collaborating with directors, essentially designing each poster iteratively: the continuous constructive criticism, creative discussions, and poster refinements helped ensure that every element of the poster contributed to a cohesive and compelling overall design.
